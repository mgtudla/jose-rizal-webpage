About

This website is part of our school project centered around the Rizal course. Here are the people who contributed to its development:

- Yhasmine Mikaela Lopez
- Jasmine Irish Marcelo
- Juliane Nicole Soriano
- Mariel Taruc
- Marielle Tudla

The contents of this website feature various artworks, images, and texts sourced from different creators and origins. They remain the sole property of their respective owners. The materials utilized on this platform are strictly intended for educational purposes.

At Rizal website, we are passionate about preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Jose Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines. Our platform serves as an educational hub, offering a diverse range of resources, insights, and discussions about Rizal's profound impact on Philippine history, literature, and society.
